Registration

Here are the participation fees for CIBB 2021:

   IEEE Member full registration: 200€
   Non-IEEE Member full registration: 250€

The registration will include the possibility to attend the conference virtually, to present one accepted short paper during the conference, and to be invited for the submission of an extended version of the accepted short paper in the post-conference publications (Springer LNBI, BMC Bioinformatics, or BMC Medical Informatics and Decision Making).
The registration is now open and users can register here on this website of Athena Consulting: https://www.athenaconsulting.eu/CIBB2021_registration/
