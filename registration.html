Registration

Here are the participation fees for CIBB 2021:

   IEEE Member full registration: 200€
   Non-IEEE Member full registration: 250€

The registration will include the possibility to attend the conference virtually, to present one accepted short paper during the conference, and to be invited for the submission of an extended version of the accepted short paper in the post-conference publications (Springer LNBI, BMC Bioinformatics, or BMC Medical Informatics and Decision Making). We will use the collected funds only to cover the conference expenses.

The registration is now open and articles' authors can register here on this website of Athena Consulting: https://www.athenaconsulting.eu/CIBB2021_registration/

The virtual attendance to the conference is free of charge for all the people not presenting an article.

Questions related to the registration and the payment can be addressed to Athena Consulting at athena(AT)athenaconsulting.eu
When contacting Athena Consulting, please include the information about the EasyChair number of your article.
