Registration

Here are the participation fees for CIBB 2021:

   IEEE Member full registration: 200€
   Non-IEEE Member full registration: 250€

The registration will include the possibility attend the conference virtually and, in case of short paper acceptance, the possibility to present one short paper during the conference, and to be invited for the submission of an extended version of the accepted short paper in the post-conference publications (Springer LNBI, BMC Bioinformatics, or BMC Biomedical Informatics).
The website for the registration and the fee payment will be online soon.
