Registration

Here are the participation fees for CIBB 2021:

   IEEE Member full registration: 200€
   Non-IEEE Member full registration: 250€

The registration will include the possibility to present a paper (in case of acceptance), and to be invited for the submission of an extended version of it in the post-conference publications (Springer LNBI, BMC Bioinformatics, or BMC Biomedical Informatics).
The website for the registration and the fee payment will be online soon.
