Conference online participation

To participate in the the conference, participants , keynote speakers, invited speakers, and all attendees can visit the CIBB 2021 website on WeConf (https://weconf.eu/cibb-2021/schedule) and then click on the red button saying JOIN LIVE SESSION, that contains a Zoom link.
